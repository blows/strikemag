Bio: Morgan is a political agitator and theorist who writes about gender, queer theory, and anarchism. He's a part of the DIY Space For London Accountability Working Group, and is currently working on trans necropolitics with The Corpse Project. He co-runs a queer boxing group and a support group for men (trans, cis, and intersex) and non-binary survivors of sexual violence. He likes plants, sci-fi, and anti-zionist Yiddish singing.
