Bio: Liv Wynter is the curator of ‘Lessons in Anti Apathy’. The purpose of these panel discussions is to bring together groups of activists to discuss how better to build and work together. This text is the keynote delivered at the second panel discussion. The first LIAA included Sisters Uncut, STRIKE! Magazine, London Palestine Action and WHEREISANAMENDIETA. The second included Movement for Justice, Anti Raids Network, Black Lives Matter UK and Goldsmiths Cut The Rent.
