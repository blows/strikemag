Aboutus: 

STRIKE! is a dissident collective led by women. We produce a quarterly magazine, video content and organise events across London. We are committed to platforming grassroots movements and the creative culture of these fights. 

STRIKE! is reader-supported and advertisement free. We aren’t funded by corporations, we’re funded by you. Help us keep publishing the important work we care about by (link: http://strikemag.bigcartel.com/product/strike-subscription text: subscribing).

Submissions: 

Each issue of STRIKE! is themed. For each issue we will release a short editorial statement on that theme that you can respond to however you like, be it written, visual, or audio, etc. 

If you would like to contribute then please send us an email with a pitch of 200 words and a proposed first paragraph to <editor@strikemag.org>. Please familiarise yourself with STRIKE!’s content to make sure this is the right platform for you.

Distribution: 

STRIKE! Magazine is a self-published and self-distributed print magazine. The current issue and back issues that are still in stock are available to buy from our online shop. We aim to fulfil postal orders once a week, shipping via Royal Mail 2nd Class, so we thank you in advance for your patience in receiving your order! 

If you have any questions about your order, want to update the address associated with your subscription, or are interesting in stocking or distributing STRIKE! Magazine, please email <distribution@strikemag.org>.
